Summary

What is Until?

Until is a savings timeline calculator for people who know what they want to buy but not how long it'll take. Enter your target item, its price, and how much you can set aside at a given frequency — daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly — and the app tells you exactly when you'll reach your goal. It also generates a full savings plan with a projected start and end date, so you can commit to a schedule rather than just guess.

Set your target

Name the item, set the price, pick a currency. That's it.

Define your pace

Choose how much you can save and how often — daily to yearly.

Get the date

Know exactly when you'll have enough, down to the day.

A note

I made this web app primarily for myself because, like many people, I spend an unreasonable amount of time looking at things I can't afford yet. 😆 Whenever I want to buy something, I usually order it online. The problem is that I also enjoy browsing products and adding them to my mental wishlist long before my allowance and savings are ready for them. Naturally, this led to the age-old question: "How many more days must I survive before I can click 'Order Now'?" So I made this app. I actually built a similar app before called FundTimer, but let's just say it had a great personality and questionable looks. So I rebuilt it, gave it a glow-up, added a few new features, and renamed it Until. That's pretty much everything you need to know (probably). Feel free to use Until and may your savings grow faster than your wishlist. God bless you. 🙏
